Access WhatsApp on your PC and Phone with WhatsApp Web Sign In


Scan Whatsapp Web QR Code for logging into your Whatsapp Account easily.

  1. Head on to web.whatsapp.com through Web browser.
  2. Tap on the Three dots at the left side of the screen.
  3. Now, tap on the “Linked Devices” option.
  4. Then, A QR Code appears now.
  5. Now, align Phone's camera to the QR code.
  6. A confirmation Code is sent to you.
  7. This will now sync your Whatsapp Web account on your desktop.

Fix Whatsapp Web QR Code Issues: For Hassle-free Connectivity

You can simply fix the Whatsapp Web QR Scan issues by going ahead with the folllowing troubleshoot tips:

  • Stable Internet connection: Ensure an active Internet connection on your device.
  • Reloading QR Code: Refresh Whatsapp Web page to generate a new QR Code.
  • Clean Camera Lens: Make sure that the Camera lens is clear.
  • Whatsapp Updated: Ensure that the Whatsapp is updated to latest version.

Upon logging into your Whatsapp Web account, you can also find out the devices connected. Simply, tap on the three dots menu, tap on 'Linked Devices', select the 'Linked devices' option to Figure out devices on which Whatsapp is logged in.


WhatsApp Login: Seamless Integration Across multiple devices

Seamless messaging service, Web Whatsapp Desktop is available with Multiple device access feature. It means web.whatsapp.com can be logged in upto 4 devices simultaneously, required the four devices are linked to Primary Phone. Web whatsapp is Secure and reliable way to access your Primary whatsapp account.

Besides, Whatsapp Web login across multiple devices eases multi-tasking and sharing of media on larger screen.

You can disconnect linked devices from Primary Phone by tapping on the Three dots Menu, then 'Linked devices'. Simply Tap on the device name, from where you wish to logout your device. On the Linked device, tap on Whatsapp Settings and click 'Logout'.
